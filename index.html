Best Place for Snorkelling in the Andamans

If you like snorkeling, then don't miss this place. Forest permit can be taken one day in advance for 50/- from Port Blair or directly at Wandoor Jetty (this may be risky as the limited number of seats may be pre-occupied). Ferry to and fro charge is 750/- with complimentary 10 min glass boat ride.
